the world’s tech problems

Collective Academy is an intensive 8-week course teaching young people how to build real world web applications. If you thrive in a collective learning environment, enjoy creative problem solving and want to acquire high value technical skills, apply now.

Project Based

The course emphasises project work building real-world applications: every week you’ll build slick websites, smart servers, and cool applications.

Self-Organising

Students take responsibility for organising themselves into agile product development teams. Learning is self-directed and driven by real world goals.

Supported

The course is publicly funded. Students pay no fees so we can be highly selective taking the most able and motivated applicants.

Bounties

Through our bounty scheme, external organisations invite our graduates to tackle their tech problems under the guidance of expert mentors.


Bounties provide organisations with no-risk, cost-effective project work and our graduates an opportunity to build a portfolio or real world projects


Are you an organisation with a demand for digital services? Do you want to support an innovative educational project helping young people gain high value skills? If so, the bounty scheme is for you.

Jobs

TWe produce junior developers who are able to build full stack web applications on RESTful APIs. They learn to code in HTML, CSS, Javascript and Node.js.


Many of our graduates go on to take up junior developer roles in tech businesses and agencies. Every course produces a clutch of new graduates looking for challenging work in organisations doing cool thing in the world.
