Model

Founders and Coders now run a 16-week programme. For the first 8 weeks, students take part in our intensive full-stack web-development course. For the second, students are invited to remain in our space,

1) where they will work on real projects, doing interesting and challenging work, while being paid for the value of work they produce.

2) where they will do real paid-projects under the guidance of mentors.

3) where they will team up with mentors to work on real projects.

Ethos

We value diversity. We aim to have an even number of men and women and take people from backgrounds that are underrepresented in the tech industry. There are no formal educational requirements; our selections are made on aptitude, work ethic and desire to have a positive impact on the world. We are building a community of exceptional people who are interested in learning, teaching and succeeding, together.

Vision

Building on the ideas of Sugata Mitra, Richard Felder and Elliot Aronson we have developed a group based educational model, nurturing passion, community and hard work. With the support of our alumni, we expect our model to be self sustaining and replicable. Education should not be an expensive and isolated experience, it should be free and communal. We want our courses, and similar projects, to be available worldwide.
