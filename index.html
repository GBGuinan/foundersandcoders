Pay it Forward

The free Founders and Coders course trains young people with no experience to be junior web developers via an intensive eight week course run several times a year. We accept applications from anyone who is willing to work hard, work in a team and keen to learn. Employers are able to hire our students with no finder's fee, our programming grads are now working in tech companies all over London. Other graduates work freelance or stick around to help us tackle projects and help pass on the skills that they have learnt here to future cohorts.
