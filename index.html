The coding course

Founders and Coders now runs a 16-week programme. For the first eight weeks, students take part in our intensive full-stack JavaScript web development course, working in groups on weekly projects. For the second eight weeks, students are invited to remain in our space, working on a variety of projects, under the guidance of mentors.

Our ethos

We value diversity. We aim to have a balance of men and women on our courses and encourage applicants from backgrounds that are under-represented in the tech industry. There are no formal educational requirements; our selections are made on aptitude, work ethic and desire to have a positive impact on the world.

Our vision

We have developed a group-based educational model, nurturing commitment, community and hard work. With the support of our alumni, we expect our model to be self-sustaining and replicable. Education should not be an expensive and isolated experience; it should be free and communal—and available everywhere.
