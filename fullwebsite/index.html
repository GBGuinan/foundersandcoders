What we do

Founders and Coders now run a 16-week programme. For the first 8 weeks, students take part in our intensive full-stack JavaScript web-development course, working in groups on weekly projects. For the second 8 weeks, students are invited to remain in our space, working on external, paid projects, under the guidance of a mentor.

Who we are

There are no formal educational requirements to apply for the course; our selections are made on aptitude and work ethic. We aim to have a balance of men and women and encourage applicants from backgrounds underrepresented in the tech industry who are interested in learning, teaching and succeeding together.

Why it matters

Education should not be an expensive and isolated experience, it should be free and communal. Building on the ideas of Sugata Mitra, Richard Felder and Elliot Aronson, we have developed a group-based learning model. With the support of our alumni and mentors, we expect our model to be self-sustaining and replicable.
