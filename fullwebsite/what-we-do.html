Bounties

Through our bounty scheme, external organisations invite our graduates to tackle their tech problems under the guidance of expert mentors.


Bounties provide organisations with no-risk, cost-effective project work. At the same time, our graduates get an opportunity to build a portfolio with real world projects.


Does your organisation need digital services? Do you want to support an innovative educational project providing free programming training? If so, the bounty scheme is for you.

Jobs

We produce junior developers who are able to build full-stack web applications on RESTful APIs. They learn to code in HTML, CSS, Javascript and Node.js and are proficient by the end of the course.


Many of our graduates go on to take up junior developer roles in tech businesses and agencies. Every course produces a group of new graduates looking for challenging work in organisations doing interesting things.


If you are recruiting junior development talent, get in touch.

Better Together

Our pool of graduates come from a variety of academic and industry backgrounds, but they have one thing in common: they are all better working together. Because the course is run entirely as group projects, working together on commercial work just feels natural. We prefer the scrum approach, which means we stay flexible as your needs change. This Agile style allows us to provide you with a functioning prototype quickly and iterate rapidly.
