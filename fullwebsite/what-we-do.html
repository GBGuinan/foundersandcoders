A helping hand with your project

Got an idea but not sure about the technical side?


Founders and Coders can help take your idea off of the sketchpad and into production. If you are not sure where to start, we can help by providing technical consultancy. Have some idea on the tech side but need muscle to make it a reality? We can help you construct a first prototype and go from there.

Try before you buy


You know that good junior developers are hard to come by. The typical recruitment process is expensive and feels rushed. Luckily, we have an alternative for you.

At F&C, external organisations invite graduates to tackle tech problems on a freelance basis. The graduates receive a small fee if the external organisation is happy with the work. At the same time, our graduates get an opportunity to build a portfolio.

Through this scheme, we provide organisations with no-risk, cost-effective project work and a chance to get to know our graduates. Smiles all around. Drop us a line if this sounds better than the recruitment circus.

Education outside of our classroom



We don’t believe learning stops beyond our four walls. That’s why we also conduct workshops at your workplace or school, and make our course material available online for free.

Would your business or students benefit from a refresher on web development and/or programming? Get in touch. We can tailor a session to your needs and interests, and work around your schedule on location and timing.

So far, we have worked with London schools, councils, universities, and a variety of companies.
