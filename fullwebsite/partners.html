We can help take your idea off of the sketchpad and into production. If you are not sure where to start, we can help by providing technical consultancy. Have some idea on the tech side but need muscle to make it a reality? We can help you construct a first prototype and go from there.

Try before you buy


You know that good junior developers are hard to come by. The typical procurement and recruitment processes are expensive and feel rushed. Luckily, we have an alternative for you.

You can invite graduates to tackle your tech problems on a freelance basis. The graduates receive a small fee if you are happy with the work. At the same time, our graduates get an opportunity to build a portfolio.

Through this scheme, we provide you with no-risk, cost-effective project work and a chance to get to know our graduates. Smiles all around. Drop us a line if this sounds better than the recruitment circus.

Education outside of our classroom



We don’t believe learning stops beyond our four walls. That’s why we can also conduct workshops at your workplace or school.
