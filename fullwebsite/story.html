The story so far

Our story began in the British Library, with a senior developer, Dan, trying to brush up on the latest technologies. However, self study can be a slow and arduous journey. With this in mind, Dan began arranging meetups, to gather together other developers, in order to create a peer-based learning environment.


As the success of these meetups grew, so did his vision for what could be accomplished within this collaborative model. By working together, these groups were powering through the material faster than ever, with a better comprehension of the material than he could have ever imagined.


When he met with Simon, the Chairman of Camden Collective, the potential for this model was quickly identified. Simon arranged a work space for Dan, and together they began working towards creating an intensive coding bootcamp for complete beginners.

Our Model

The course's self-funding model will have two main streams of income: renting workspace to our graduates (and other developers), and sponsorship from companies who wish to employ them. The Indiegogo campaign we are running will cover the first three months rent to allow graduates to develop themselves professionally. After that, they will be able to fund the space themselves while volunteering time to help teach the next course.
